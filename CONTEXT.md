# Private Office

The Private Office is the authenticated client and administrative workspace for engagements, documents, communication, billing, and approvals.

## Language

**Client**:
The person or organization receiving advisory services and access to a Private Office.
_Avoid_: Customer, account

**Engagement**:
The defined advisory matter connecting a client, property, work phase, documents, and billing.
_Avoid_: Project, job

**Invoice**:
The original request for payment issued for an engagement.
_Avoid_: Bill, payment request

**Contract Document**:
An uploaded agreement that records terms accepted or awaiting signature for an engagement.
_Avoid_: Contract record, deal

**Change Order**:
A separately numbered amendment linked to one original invoice or Contract Document; it records a scope or price change without rewriting the original.
_Avoid_: Invoice edit, contract edit, adjustment

**Original Amount**:
The amount stated on an Invoice when it was issued, excluding later Change Orders.
_Avoid_: Base price

**Revised Total**:
The Original Amount plus the financial deltas from approved Change Orders.
_Avoid_: New invoice amount, adjusted invoice
